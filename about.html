Hi,
 
I'm Charlie Calvert and I'm your teacher.

I've been working with computers and writing code in one form or another for about 30 years. I started out with Turbo Pascal and Delphi, spent time with Java, C++, Python, Perl, and C#.  I've spent most of my career in industry, working first for Borland, then for a start-up in Silicon Valley, and for a few years, I was at Microsoft on the C# team as a Technical Evangelist. Over the last few years, I've been interested primarily in Cloud and Mobile Development with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

In this class, I'm looking forward to exploring the ways we write web and mobile applications that use Cloud services. I'm also looking to learn new ways to use virtual machines, cloud resources, and mobile devices.
 
I enjoy hiking, snow-shoeing, swimming, reading and watching movies. I also like to write. I'm known in the computer world primarily as a book author:
